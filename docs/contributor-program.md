# KubeSynapse Contributor Program

We believe contributors are the heart of open source. The KubeSynapse Contributor Program recognizes, rewards, and elevates community members who invest their time and expertise.

## Recognition Tiers

### 🥉 Bronze Contributor (1+ merged PR)
- Name listed in CONTRIBUTORS.md
- "KubeSynapse Contributor" badge for GitHub profile
- Shoutout in release notes

### 🥈 Silver Contributor (5+ merged PRs)
- All Bronze benefits
- KubeSynapse t-shirt mailed to you
- Early access to roadmap and RFCs
- Eligible for "Committer" role (merge rights)

### 🥇 Gold Contributor (15+ merged PRs, or significant feature)
- All Silver benefits
- KubeSynapse hoodie mailed to you
- Spotlight interview on kubesynapse.ai blog
- Conference ticket sponsorship (1/year, up to $2,000)
- Eligible for Maintainer role

### 💎 Platinum Contributor (Sustained leadership, 30+ PRs, or community pillar)
- All Gold benefits
- KubeSynapse jacket + custom swag box
- Named in README.md as core contributor
- KubeCon/CNCF travel sponsorship (1/year, up to $3,000)
- Eligible for TOC seat
- KubeSynapse credit on your profile and resume

## How to Earn Recognition

Recognition is based on sustained, quality contributions. Every merged PR counts, but we particularly value:

### Code Contributions
- Feature implementation
- Bug fixes
- Performance improvements
- Test coverage expansion
- Security vulnerability fixes

### Non-Code Contributions
- Documentation improvements
- Tutorial and blog post writing
- Community support (answering issues, Discord/Slack help)
- Bug triage and reproduction
- Conference talks and workshops about KubeSynapse
- UX/design contributions

### Special Recognition Categories

| Category | Criteria | Reward |
|----------|----------|--------|
| **Bug Hunter** | Find and report 3+ valid security vulnerabilities | Gold tier + branded swag |
| **Doc Champion** | 10+ significant documentation PRs | Silver tier + Docs contributor badge |
| **Community Hero** | 50+ helpful responses in community channels | Silver tier |
| **First PR** | Your first merged contribution | Bronze tier + digital badge |

## Nomination Process

1. **Self-nomination:** Open an issue with title `[Contributor Program] Nomination: @yourhandle`
2. **Peer nomination:** Anyone can nominate using the same format
3. Maintainers review nominations monthly during the community call
4. Recognition is awarded retroactively — your work speaks for itself

## Contributor Spotlight

Every month, we feature one contributor on the [KubeSynapse blog](https://kubesynapse.ai/blog):

**Format:**
- Short interview (5 questions about your KubeSynapse journey)
- Photo and bio
- Links to your contributions
- What you're building with KubeSynapse

To be featured, open an issue with title `[Contributor Spotlight] @yourhandle` and answer the interview questions.

## Swag Delivery

We ship swag worldwide! After you reach a tier:
1. Fill out the swag form (shared in your recognition issue)
2. Provide your shipping address and size preferences
3. Allow 2-4 weeks for production and delivery

## Conference Sponsorship

Gold and Platinum contributors can request sponsorship for one conference per year:
- **Gold:** Up to $2,000 for ticket + travel
- **Platinum:** Up to $3,000 for ticket + travel + accommodation

Submit a request via GitHub issue with title `[Sponsorship] Conference Name - @yourhandle` at least 4 weeks before the event.

## Maintaining Your Status

- Recognition tiers are cumulative (you don't lose them)
- Active contribution (> 1 PR/year) maintains eligibility for conference sponsorship
- Inactive contributors retain their badge and listing but may not receive new swag
