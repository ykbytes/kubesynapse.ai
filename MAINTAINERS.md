## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Review and merge pull requests following the project's [contribution guidelines](CONTRIBUTING.md)
- Triage and respond to issues within 5 business days
- Define and communicate the project roadmap (see [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md))
- Ensure the project adheres to the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Manage releases and versioning following [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/)
- Handle security vulnerability reports per [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)
- Maintain CI/CD pipelines and build infrastructure

## Becoming a Maintainer

Maintainers are appointed based on:

1. **Sustained Contribution**: At least 5 merged pull requests of non-trivial quality
2. **Community Engagement**: Active participation in issue triage, code reviews, and discussions
3. **Domain Expertise**: Deep understanding of Kubernetes, AI agents, or the project's architecture
4. **Alignment**: Demonstrated commitment to the project's values and goals

To nominate yourself or someone else, open a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/ykbytes/kubemininions/discussions) with the `maintainer-nomination` label.
